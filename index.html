About

Background

I'm a trilingual data scientist and ML engineer with three years of industry experience at JLL in Guangzhou, where I built LLM pipelines, OCR scrapers, and computer vision models applied to street-view imagery.

My work sits at the intersection of applied ML, search systems, and autonomous perception. I'm particularly drawn to problems involving video, temporal modelling, and multi-modal data — which led me to my UBC capstone project MooVision, a real-time dairy calf behavior detection system using YOLOv8, ByteTrack, and LSTM.

Outside of work I follow autonomous vehicles and robotics closely, play golf badly, and have three cats who have no opinions about machine learning.
